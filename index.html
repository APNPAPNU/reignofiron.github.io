About Us

Reign of Iron is a clan playing Destiny 2 on PC. We primarily focus on PvP gameplay, but also spend a fair amount of time on PvE endgame content, like raids and prestige Nightfalls.

Why Reign of Iron?

Reign of Iron is a mid-sized clan with a healthy active roster full of people who still enjoy this game. Our goal is to make friends and help others enjoy this game too. Our main focus is PvP and ultimately consistent success in Trials, as well as building a Prestige Raid team.

Our members are strong players, but we're not gods. That means that you don't have to be a god to join (but if you are, awesome), but we are not a carry clan.

No K/D requirements. No required hours of operation. Our only requirement is that you have Discord and use it. Just know what you're doing out there, be willing to make callouts and play as a team, and strive to improve every week. That's what we're doing too.
